Phishing Prevention & Awareness Guidelines

DO’s:
- Verify the sender’s email address before taking action
- Hover over links to check the actual URL
- Be cautious of urgent or threatening messages
- Report suspicious emails to the IT or security team
- Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)

DON’Ts:
- Do not click on unknown or suspicious links
- Do not share passwords or OTPs through email
- Do not download attachments from unknown senders
- Do not panic or rush when reading security alerts
